Double DePauw Dice Game

Welcome to the Double DePauw Dice Game!

In the black dice, 'D' (DePauw) stands for +1, 'W' (Wabash) for -1, and '-' (Blank) for 0. The gold dice either multiplies the black 'D's by 2 if it's a 'D', the black 'W's by 2 if it's a 'W', or does nothing if it's a '-'.

The player with the highest score after the selected rounds wins. Good luck!
